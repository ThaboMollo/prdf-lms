## • Key Eligibility Criteria
Consideration will be given to the following applicants:
o Applicants who demonstrate expected Developmental Impact
o Projects must demonstrate targets for employment creation
o Enterprises must be >50.1% black women owned
o Applicants must be 90% South African nationals with operations controlled by SA
citizens
o Enterprises must be 100% Director Operational
o Applicants must be willing to participate in developmental programs
o Transactions from rural provinces must have rural community participation
o Projects must demonstrate sustainability
o Applicants must be permanent residents of South Africa
o The Enterprise(s) must be compliant with generally accepted corporate governance
practices appropriate to the client’s legal status
o The business must demonstrate capacity to repay the loan offered
o The business must be registered with the CIPC
o The business must be registered with SARS as a taxpayer and in possession of a valid
tax clearance certificate or a tax pin
o The members/shareholders of the business must not be un-rehabilitated insolvents
and not be under debt review or an administration order
