### Addressing Your Key Concerns

We want to directly address the priorities Mandisa highlighted recently:

1. **Turnaround Times (The 5-Day SLA):** We know that speed is critical. We are actively building a tracking system that will visually warn your team if an application is sitting idle. This will help you enforce the 5-day turnaround time without having to manually check every application.
2. **Non-Financial Support:** We recognize that PRDF provides crucial advisory support, not just funding. We are building a dedicated section in the system for your advisors to record and track the non-financial support they provide to clients.
3. **Language Flexibility:** Accessibility is a priority. While the current system is in English, we have built the foundation to easily plug in local languages (like isiZulu and Sesotho) in the very next phase, ensuring rural clients can navigate the platform comfortably.
