# PRDF Client Portal — Demo Script & Storyboard

A ~2.5–3 minute walkthrough of the **PRDF client-facing loan portal**: from a
prospective business owner discovering PRDF, checking eligibility, applying for
funding, and tracking their application.

**Persona:** *Thabo Mponya*, director of *Brightfields Trading (Pty) Ltd*, a
black‑women‑owned fresh‑produce distributor in a Gauteng township, seeking
R250,000 to expand delivery capacity.

**Assets in this folder**
- `video/prdf-client-walkthrough.webm` — screen recording of the full journey
- `screenshots/01…21` — retina stills for each scene (use in slides/thumbnails)

---

## Voiceover script (with on‑screen actions)

> Timings are approximate. Narration is written to be read at a relaxed pace.
> Each scene lists the matching screenshot for a slide-based version.

### Scene 1 — The landing page  ·  `01-landing-hero`, `03-landing-full`
**Action:** Open the homepage. Slowly scroll through the hero, About, Eligibility,
How‑It‑Works, Support and Documents sections.

> "Meet PRDF — the People's Republic Development Fund. It's a developmental
> finance institution that gives South African SMEs access to affordable loans,
> from R10,000 up to R10 million. The homepage tells the whole story at a glance:
> who qualifies, how the process works, the support services on offer, and exactly
> which documents you'll need — so there are no surprises later."

### Scene 2 — Instant loan calculator  ·  `02-landing-calculator`
**Action:** Drag the amount slider up; the monthly repayment updates live.

> "Right from the hero, a prospective borrower can model a loan. As Thabo adjusts
> the amount and term, the estimated monthly repayment updates instantly — setting
> expectations before he even creates an account."

### Scene 3 — Eligibility self‑assessment  ·  `04-eligibility-empty`, `05-eligibility-filled`
**Action:** Go to *Check Eligibility*. Tick each criterion across the three
sections — Ownership, Compliance, and Business Viability. The counter climbs.

> "Before applying, borrowers run a quick self‑assessment. It's grouped into
> ownership and structure, registration and compliance, and business viability.
> The progress counter makes it clear how many criteria are met."

### Scene 4 — You qualify  ·  `06-eligibility-result`
**Action:** Click *Check My Eligibility*. The result page shows a green tick and
an eligibility summary.

> "Because Brightfields meets every requirement, Thabo gets an immediate,
> encouraging result — with a clear summary and a single call to action:
> create an account and apply."

### Scene 5 — Create account / Sign in  ·  `07-register`, `08-login`
**Action:** Show the split‑screen register form, then the login screen. Sign in.

> "Registration takes under two minutes. Returning clients simply sign in to pick
> up where they left off. Notice the branded panel — and yes, the PRDF logo now
> takes you straight back home."

### Scene 6 — Client home / dashboard  ·  `18-home`
**Action:** Land on the client home after login.

> "Once inside, the client portal greets Thabo by name. His dashboard summarises
> everything that matters: his active application and its status, how many
> applications he's submitted, active loans, outstanding balance, and a feed of
> recent activity."

### Scene 7 — Application status tracker  ·  `19-status`
**Action:** Open *Status* from the sidebar.

> "The status page turns an opaque process into a transparent one. A step‑by‑step
> tracker shows exactly where the application sits — here it's *Under Review* — all
> the way through approval, disbursement, repayment and closure."

### Scene 8 — Documents  ·  `20-documents`
**Action:** Open *Documents*. Show the required‑documents checklist and uploader.

> "Every application needs supporting documents. The portal lists each one, tracks
> what's still outstanding, and lets clients drag‑and‑drop uploads against the
> selected application — no email back‑and‑forth."

### Scene 9 — The application wizard  ·  `09`→`13`
**Action:** Open *Apply*. Walk through the five steps:
1. `10-apply-step1-filled` — Business & compliance profile
2. `11-apply-step2-financials` — Financials
3. `12-apply-step3-loan` — Loan details (with live debt breakdown)
4. `13-apply-step4-documents` — Document uploads
5. `14-apply-step5-review` — Review

> "Applying is a guided, five‑step wizard. Step one captures the business profile
> and compliance flags. Step two, the financials. Step three, the loan itself —
> with a live debt breakdown showing the monthly instalment and total repayable.
> Step four collects documents, and step five lets Thabo review everything before
> submitting."

### Scene 10 — Consent & submit  ·  `15-apply-consent-modal`, `16-apply-consent-filled`
**Action:** Click *Submit Application*. The POPIA consent modal appears; answer
YES to each acknowledgement.

> "Before anything is submitted, PRDF captures explicit, POPIA‑compliant consent —
> data processing, credit checks, and policy acknowledgements — each recorded
> individually. Only once every item is acknowledged can the application be
> submitted for review."

### Scene 11 — Close  ·  `01-landing-hero` (or logo)
> "From first click to submitted application, PRDF gives South African business
> owners a fast, transparent, fully online path to funding — and the tools to
> track every step of the journey. That's the PRDF client portal."

## Notes & caveats for whoever records the final cut

- **Demo account:** `thabo@brightfields.co.za` / `DemoPassw0rd!` (a confirmed
  Supabase user seeded for this demo, with one application in *Under Review*).
- **Submission is intentionally not completed on camera.** In the current hosted
  Supabase project the `public.application_consents` table is missing, so a real
  submit fails with *"Could not find the table 'public.application_consents'"*.
  The walkthrough shows the consent step and stops there. Apply the consent
  migration to that project to enable an end‑to‑end submit on camera.
- **My Loans** shows an empty state for this client because no loan has been
  disbursed (and the `loans` table's row‑level security blocks a client from
  reading loan rows directly). Populating it end‑to‑end needs a disbursed loan
  plus an RLS policy that lets clients read their own loans.
- **Video format:** the recording is `.webm` (VP8). It plays in any modern
  browser, Slack, and Google Drive. For an `.mp4` (e.g. PowerPoint/Keynote),
  re‑encode with a full ffmpeg: `ffmpeg -i prdf-client-walkthrough.webm -c:v libx264 -pix_fmt yuv420p prdf-client-walkthrough.mp4`.
- Screenshots are captured at 2× (retina), 1440×900 viewport, full‑page.
